## QUALITY CHECKLIST (Test Batch)

When you receive the 10 test copies, verify:

### Visual Quality
- [ ] Colors match your brand (Blue: #2563EB, Orange: #F97316)
- [ ] Logo is sharp and clear
- [ ] No pixelation or blurriness
- [ ] Text is readable (minimum 10pt font clear)
- [ ] Alignment is correct (no cropping of important content)
- [ ] Both sides printed correctly
- [ ] No smudging or printing errors

### Paper Quality
- [ ] Feels premium (300gsm should feel thick/sturdy)
- [ ] Lamination smooth (no bubbles or wrinkles)
- [ ] Corners not damaged
- [ ] No discoloration
- [ ] Consistent thickness across all 10 copies

### QR Code Testing
- [ ] All 4 QR codes scan successfully
- [ ] Scan from 30cm distance (arm's length)
- [ ] Works on iPhone (test with Camera app)
- [ ] Works on Android (test with Camera app)
- [ ] Links redirect to correct URLs
- [ ] UTM parameters captured in analytics
- [ ] Still scans after bending/folding pamphlet

### Content Verification
- [ ] Phone number correct
- [ ] Email address correct
- [ ] Website URLs correct
- [ ] Offer expiry date correct
- [ ] No spelling errors
- [ ] All checkmarks (âœ“) and icons visible
- [ ] QR code labels match their links
