- Journey metaphor = growth mindset

- Each entrepreneur finds their stage
- Multiple entry points (business or personal)
- Can engage with one or all solutions
- QR codes let them choose their path

**4. Trust Building**
- Real testimonials with names/locations
- Specific results (â‚¹12L saved, 90 days)
- Fortune 500 credibility
- 50+ businesses social proof

**5. Emotional Resonance**
- Family safety (FamilyKnows) = powerful motivator
- Business growth anxiety addressed
- Peace of mind emphasized
- Journey language = hope and progress
